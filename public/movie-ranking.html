Indonesia's Best Horror Movie Ever Created

Top 3 Scariest Indonesian Movie of All Time


Satan's Slave (2017)

A little bit jumpscare. But the movie's settings and the story line will make us shiver.

May The Devil Take You (2018)

The storyline is unique and fascinating. It perfectly pictures the highclass Indonesian while struggling in business and intentionally chose the wrong path to solve his problem. The devil's appearance is one of the most scariest and disturbest among Indonesian evil.

May The Devil Take You Too (2020)

The prequel of 2018's box office May The Devil Take You Too. Most of the storyline in this movie is adventure-type movie, mostly set in orphanage while the story is the continuation of the previous line.
